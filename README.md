# 📘 MBA Thesis: COVID-19 Effects in the Textile Industry

## 📌 Overview
This repository contains my MBA graduation project completed at Yildiz Technical University.

The study examines the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on the textile and apparel industry, with a focus on Turkey and a case analysis of Mavi.

---

## 🎯 Scope of the Study
- Overview of the textile and apparel sector
- Impact of COVID-19 on global and Turkish textile markets
- Analysis of changes in sales, demand, and supply chains
- Case study: Financial analysis of Mavi

---

## 🧠 Key Findings
- COVID-19 significantly disrupted global textile demand
- Supply chain disruptions created both risks and opportunities
- Turkey maintained production capacity and gained strategic importance
- Mavi demonstrated financial resilience despite the crisis
