# 📊 COVID-19 Impact on the Textile Sector: Financial Analysis of Mavi

## 📌 Overview
This project is based on my MBA research and has been transformed into a Python-based data analysis project.

It analyzes the financial impact of COVID-19 on the Turkish textile sector, focusing on Mavi Giyim. The study combines financial statement analysis with sector-level insights to evaluate company performance during a global crisis.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze Mavi’s financial performance during COVID-19 (2019–2020)
- Evaluate revenue, cost, and profitability trends
- Examine balance sheet changes (assets vs liabilities)
- Assess the impact of COVID-19 on the textile sector
- Compare company performance with industry trends

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python
- pandas
- matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📊 Key Analyses

### 1. Income Statement Analysis
- Revenue increased by 21.7%
- Cost of Goods Sold increased by 27.4%
- EBITDA increased significantly by 75.5%

👉 Indicates strong operational efficiency despite rising costs.

---

### 2. Profitability Analysis
- Margins remained relatively stable
- Cost pressure affected net profit growth

---

### 3. Balance Sheet Analysis
- Total assets and liabilities increased significantly
- Growth supported by increased financial obligations

---

### 4. Sector Analysis
- Turkey’s share in EU textile imports slightly declined (3.9% → 3.6%)
- Global demand disruptions affected the sector

---

### 5. Company vs Sector Comparison
- Textile sector experienced contraction during COVID-19
- Mavi demonstrated strong financial growth

👉 Shows company-level resilience and competitive advantage

## ⚠️ Limitations
- Analysis is based on limited time periods (2019–2020)
- Some sector data is not directly comparable
- Results should be interpreted directionally

---

## 🧠 Key Insight
Mavi demonstrated strong financial resilience during the COVID-19 period. Despite increased costs, the company maintained profitability and significantly improved operational efficiency, outperforming broader industry trends.
